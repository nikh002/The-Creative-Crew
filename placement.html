Our Training & Placement

The Placement cell works towards the objective of placing the students on the right career path and with sole purpose of facilitating the students in the “Job Hunt” process. The main motto of any professional college is to educate the students and make them industry worthy. Now-a-days the colleges have taken it further, as to provide placements to the students. Our college too has taken up the responsibility placement and in view of that, we have a very effective Training & Placement cell. The Training & Placement cell is nurtured by Ms. S. M Vasekar. He has a good industrial experience and with that tool he is getting many reputed companies in and around the city to come and recruit our students. The placement committee under the guidance of him undertakes various placement activities. It comprises of counseling, motivation, training and interaction with top management & students for final placement.

The objectives of TPO Cell:

To build the bridge between industry & academia. Organizing pre-placement activities. To have good relations with recruiters. To create awareness among the students about real life problems in industry. Planning& organization various placement drives in campus.
